Welcome to AdoptIQ
AdoptIQ is your adoption intelligence platform — purpose-built for OCM professionals managing enterprise-scale change. Track readiness gates, measure ADKAR, score stakeholder adoption risk, and generate executive-ready reports.
⚙
How It Works
Releases represent deployment waves (e.g. "6/21 Release").

Projects live inside releases — each with its own gate tracker, ADKAR assessment, dependency heatmap, stakeholder scoring, and resource assignments.

Export branded PDF or text summaries for executive stakeholders at any time.
